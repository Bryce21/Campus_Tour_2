Welcome to Wentworth Institute of Technology

A leader in engineering, technology, design, and management education, Wentworth provides the technical skills and professional knowledge students need to become leaders in rewarding and successful careers—and moreover, engaged citizens.

At Wentworth, you'll learn to not only master your area of expertise but also to collaborate with other students and professionals from across the university and region.

You'll benefit from our interdisciplinary curriculum, which combines rigorous studies with practical learning opportunities, and spend hours in our leading-edge labs and studios bring your ideas to life.
