Parking

Wentworth has multiple parking lots, three mostly for student use; East, Annex and Parker lot are all mixed student/professor.

Though Wentworth sells parking passes, you can also take public transportation. T-passes are sold through the info-hub or money can be added to train cards directly at stations.
